About Go Freek

Go Freek stands as an undeniable force in Australia's electronic music landscape, renowned as the architect of Gutter Tech, a raw, genre-bending sound marked by gritty basslines, industrial textures, and precision-engineered percussion. Since joining the Sweat It Out family in 2013, he has carved out a distinct sonic identity, pushing the boundaries of dance music while captivating audiences worldwide. Emerging from Wollongong's underground scene, Go Freek's journey reflects a decade-long evolution that blends underground grit with mainstage euphoria.

A defining moment came in 2015 with Define, his breakout collaboration with Dom Dolla, which dominated the ARIA Club Chart at #1 for four consecutive weeks, landed at #2 on the ARIA Club End of Year Chart, and earned an ARIA gold certification. The single has since amassed nearly 100 million streams globally, with 78.6 million on Spotify alone, establishing Go Freek's production prowess. Standout releases like Call Out on Insomniac Records/Interscope and an official remix of Deadmau5's Channel 43 on mau5trap showcase his versatility, while recent collaborations with artists such as Dope Earth Alien and Lazarusman expand his Gutter Tech vision into new territories.

On stage, Go Freek commands global festival giants like EDC Las Vegas and Beyond Wonderland, delivering innovative Freekquency sets that fuse raw energy with euphoric highs. His boundary-pushing sound has earned backing from industry titans including Diplo, FISHER, CamelPhat, Claude VonStroke, and Solomun, with roots in Sweat It Out remaining the heartbeat of his artistry. With highly anticipated 2025 releases generating buzz, Go Freek continues redefining modern dance music, delivering bold, authentic sounds that leave an indelible mark on the global electronic landscape.

What is Gutter Tech?

Gutter Tech is Go Freek's signature genre, born from the raw underbelly of electronic music, blending industrial edge with dancefloor precision to create a sound that's gritty yet euphoric. This innovative style emerged from his Sweat It Out roots, evolving through years of experimentation to capture the tension between underground authenticity and festival-ready impact. At its core, Gutter Tech redefines tech house by infusing distorted bass, mechanical rhythms, and tribal elements that resonate with modern club culture.

  • Gritty Basslines: Deep, distorted low-ends that evoke urban decay, providing the foundational drive for immersive sets.
  • Industrial Textures: Layered metallic percussion and atmospheric noise, adding raw, mechanical intensity to builds and drops.
  • Precision-Engineered Percussion: Tight, engineered rhythms that fuse tribal influences with tech precision, ensuring relentless energy on the dancefloor.
